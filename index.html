Support driven, an online community of support professionals, is bringing you a new kind of conference for people who do support. Unlike other conferences that focus on strategy and processes, this one is focused on learning and applying skills used in support.

Because everyone learns a little differently, we're organizing the conference into three parts:

Talks

Feel like being inspired? Talented speakers present new ideas and strategies to take back to work with you.

Workshops

Practice makes perfect? Learn new skills with interactive activities and guided exercises.

Unconference

Learn best by sharing ideas with peers? Choose topics in the morning and gather to discuss them throughout the day.

Share your knowledge

Have a great idea for a talk or workshop?
